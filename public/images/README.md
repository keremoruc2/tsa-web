# TSA TWENTE Logo

Place your association logo here as `logo.jpg`.

## Requirements:
- **Filename:** `logo.jpg`
- **Format:** JPEG (preferred for photos/complex images)
- **Shape:** Square (will be displayed as a circle)
- **Recommended size:** At least 256x256 pixels for crisp display

## Usage:
The logo will appear in:
- Homepage hero section (128x128px, circular)
- Navigation bar (40x40px, circular)
- Footer (40x40px, circular)

## Tips:
- Make sure the main content of your logo is centered
- Avoid important content at the corners (they will be cropped in circle display)
- Use a high-quality image for best results
